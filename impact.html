Our
Impact

Making a difference, one life at a time

OGCF might be a new organization, but our passion and commitment to helping widows and children in need is unwavering. We are constantly striving to make a positive difference in the lives of those we serve, and we are proud of the impact we have made so far.

Through our educational support programs, we have been able to provide school supplies and scholarships to children who may not have otherwise been able to attend school

Through our vocational training programs, we have given widows and children the skills they need to succeed in the workforce and provide for themselves and their families

Through our counseling and support programs, we have provided emotional and psychological support to those who are struggling with grief and loss.

As a startup with limited outreach so far, we are excited to grow our impact and help even more widows and children in need. Thank you for taking the time to learn about our organization and our mission. Together, we can make a difference.
