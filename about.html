Join us in spreading hope

Welcome to Owa's Global Charity Foundation, a multinational charity organization dedicated to empowering widows and children around the world. Our mission is to create a world where widows, children, and the less privileged have access to basic needs and opportunities for sustainable growth.

At OGCF, we believe that everyone deserves a chance to live a fulfilling life, no matter their circumstances. We are committed to providing widows and children with the tools and resources they need to overcome obstacles and achieve their full potential. We collaborate with other charities, NGOs, and government agencies to maximize our impact and reach as many people as possible.

Vision

We envision a world where every woman and child has access to the resources they need to thrive, are free from poverty, discrimination, and violence. We believe that through our collective efforts, we can create a future where everyone has the opportunity to live with dignity and achieve their dreams.

Mission

Our mission is to provide hope, support, and resources to vulnerable women and children in need, empowering them to live with dignity, and achieve their full potential.

Our Story

Building hope from the ground up

At OGCF, we believe that every person deserves the opportunity to live a fulfilling life.

Our journey began with a simple desire to help those in need, particularly widows and children who are often marginalized and forgotten.

Through our passion and dedication, we have grown from a small group of individuals with a shared vision to a community of changemakers who are making a real difference in the lives of those we serve.

While we are a relatively new organization, we have already made a significant impact in the lives of those we serve. We have seen widows and children who were once hopeless and helpless, now thriving and empowered. Our journey has just begun, and we remain committed to making a lasting difference in our community and beyond.

Owa Ihiabe

President

Mr. Owa John Ihiabe is an accomplished young professional with a diverse range of qualifications and expertise. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Political Science and International Relations, a Postgraduate degree in French, as well as two Master's degrees in Intelligence and Security, and International Relations. Additionally, he has obtained professional certifications in Criminology, Security & Terrorism, and Security Management & Executive Protection.

Beyond his impressive academic achievements, Mr. Ihiabe has a genuine passion for various interests. Alongside his dedication to his work, he finds joy in playing football and basketball, as well as engaging in security surveys and watching documentaries. Coming from a prominent political family in Nigeria, Mr. Ihiabe has been deeply influenced by his upbringing. His drive to establish the OGCF (Organization for Global Community Foundation) stems from his inherent empathy, which he attributes to his father. The plight of the less privileged and widows in Nigeria and other parts of the world has weighed heavily on his heart since a young age. Having interacted with individuals from diverse social backgrounds, ranging from domestic workers to influential politicians and successful business moguls, he is motivated to embark on a genuine and impactful mission to bring positive change to the world. Mr. Ihiabe's unwavering commitment to this cause is rooted in his deep sense of purpose and faith.
