Who We Are

WE ARE A NON-PROFIT CHARITY + NGO ORGANIZATION

At OGCF, we are dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of those we serve. We are a non-profit charity and NGO organization committed to providing support and resources to widows and children in need. Our mission is to create a world where every individual has access to the resources they need to live healthy, happy lives. Our team is made up of passionate individuals who are committed to our cause. We believe that by working together, we can create real change in the world. We are dedicated to transparency, accountability, and making a difference in the communities we serve.
